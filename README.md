AI Job Market Dashboard
📌 Overview
The AI Job Market Dashboard is an interactive data analytics project designed to explore trends in the artificial intelligence job market. The dashboard provides insights into job roles, salary distributions, required skills, experience levels, and industry demand, helping users understand current hiring trends in the AI sector.
🚀 Features


Interactive dashboard for AI job market analysis


Job role and demand analysis


Salary trend visualization


Skills and technology requirements analysis


Experience level distribution


Dynamic charts and filters for data exploration
